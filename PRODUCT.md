# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Personal finance users who want a clean, frictionless way to log and view their daily expenses. Typically busy individuals on-the-go who prefer writing/saying a quick sentence (e.g., "Lunch $15") instead of navigating complex multi-input forms.

## Product Purpose
Capital Tracker exists to make tracking daily capital and personal expenses as frictionless as possible through natural language processing (NLP) and clean visualizations. Success is defined by financial clarity and peace of mind rather than accounting complexity.

## Brand Personality
- **Serene**: Soft, breathable, and Apple Health-inspired.
- **Precise**: Clean, geometric typography (Manrope) and structure.
- **Approachable**: Financial wellness over financial auditing.

## Anti-references
- Neon-accented dark SaaS dashboards.
- Gray text on gray backgrounds with low contrast.
- Nested cards and cluttered multi-column layouts.
- Complicated, button-heavy manual input forms.

## Design Principles
1. **Frictionless Logging**: Prioritize NLP text input and instant feedback over manual form fields.
2. **Breathable Calm**: Use generous whitespace, soft tonal layers, and a unified background to reduce cognitive strain.
3. **High Contrast Legibility**: Ensure all copy is clearly readable (no low-contrast grey-on-grey text) to maintain high accessibility.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG AA compliance (contrast ratio >= 4.5:1 for standard text).
- Responsive, fluid layouts that adapt clean typography without overlapping.
- Motion respect (support `prefers-reduced-motion` and avoid looping/breathing animations).
